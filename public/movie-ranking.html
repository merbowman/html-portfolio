The Best Movies According to Meredith

My top 3 movies of all-time.

PURPLE RAIN

For me this movie represents, the greatness of the 1980's and my discovery of music. Music that was mine, not my parents. Music that spoke to me. Prince created a movie musical masterpiece that will never be surpassed.

VIVA LAS VEGAS

Elvis at his visual best. The movie plot is nothing great but it showcases EP and Ann-Margaret with singing and dancing magic. It's truly great to see them play off one another's abilities. Plus both were easy on the eyes.

COMING TO AMERICA

This movie never grows old. Eddie Murphy at his finest in multiple roles.
